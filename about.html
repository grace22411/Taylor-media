ABOUT US

Robert Taylor Media is a Communications Agency that specializes in Media Relations, PR Services and Advisory, Executive Profiling, Reputation & Crisis Management, Special Events, Hospitality and Branding.

Operating for over 12 years, we offer consummate PR, communications and media services in 2 distinct business modules; Corporate and Contemporary. This strategy enables us to approach each client distinctively, and apply solutions specific to their needs.

OUR VISION

To be Africa’s leading Creative Communications Agency.

OUR MISSION

We draw on our innovative techniques, extensive experience and unparalleled networks to support our clients’ objectives and enable them to communicate strategically with confidence, eloquence and clarity.
